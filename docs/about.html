About

Jeannine C. Alvaré MSSW, LCSW is a licensed clinical social worker who has been in private practice for over 30 years.

Jeannine has experience and insight as a practitioner and administrator of mental health programs in communities and schools within the Philadelphia area. As a mother of three children, she has first hand knowledge about the complexities of being a parent of children with special needs.

Jeannine's love and integration of nature can be discovered with her pygmy goats, bonsai trees and her ongoing commitment to cultivating and preserving green spaces.
